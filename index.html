Saucy Server

The Saucy Server Website is a web store/news page/business for a Minecraft server a girl nicknamed Saucy ran. That was the intended use of the website anyway. I wanted the website to be a place for the political news of the political movements across the server. I even made a part where all the candidates had a place to display what they stood for. I also wanted the in-game shops to have an opportunity to show what they were selling. The business idea was quite good and would have made me rich. In-game, of course. All my clients were to pay me monthly for a place on the webpage. A day or two after I deployed the first version of the webpage, I heard that two of my friends had been telling everyone that no one should cooperate with me. I left the server a few days later, and the website remains.

Kolener

Kolener is a fun little HTML challenge I set for myself. The challenge aimed to create a sort of search engine template. I went with a darkish design because I simply believe it looks better. I also advocate light mode being a horrible choice for anything that shouldn't look boring. I'm pretty proud of what I made. I think that Google should pay attention to how well everything turned out on the webpage. A detail, however, is that the navigation bar on the top looks tiny. Most of my websites before this one has very small navbars. Otherwise, I believe it's a work of art worthy of all good-looking search engine prizes.
